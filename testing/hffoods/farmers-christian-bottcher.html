This is

Christian's Story

Christian is our Arcott sheep farmer. He farms healthy cuts of 100% Organic lamb!

After much research, he realized that processed foods and preservatives were making his son sick, but that the organic food they grew in their garden did not cause a reaction. He also noticed how sick he had become from growing their own canola seeds which were regularly sprayed with pesticides.

Christian's

Arcott Sheep

The Canadian Arcott sheep are a mixture between the Ile de France and Suffolk breeds. These sheep are medium in size and relatively low maintenance. The lamb meat is described as having a good meat-to-bone ratio.
