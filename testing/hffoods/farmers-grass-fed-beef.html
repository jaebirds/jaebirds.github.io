Jill Schenk

Jill’s farming dream came true when her and her husband bought their 25 acre country property. Her husband Cody, a dairy farmer, was well-versed in daily farming activities. Jill and her children received hands-on training and learnt how to handle and care for the animals.

Meet

Andrea Schantz

Andrea and her husband Rick, along with her father Steve raise Aberdeen Angus. They purchased these grass-fed beauties from their neighbours in 2010. They saw it as a great opportunity to raise organic animals and have been researching and learning a lot as they go! They truly believe that organic is better, the way things should be. They keep the family farm tradition going. They love working outside and share a very close relationship with their cows.

Meet

Ray

Ray raises Charolais and Limousin breeds. To him, organic farming really compliments the whole picture; his cattle are a great part of the farm experience producing fertile soil and a healthy natural environment. Ray is happy when he can be outside in nature knowing he is doing everything he can to keep his cows happy and healthy! Ray works everyday to help reclaiming the farming experience that has been lost long ago. A small farm making a big difference!

Meet

Derek

Derek raises Angus. He has been in the cattle business for 20 years and has been raising his cattle organically for 17 years. He loves being the farmer; working with the animals and growing things. Derek farms with his parents, wife and his 4 kids. They run an 850 acre farm, every acre certified organic!
