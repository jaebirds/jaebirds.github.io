Can I pick up my order instead of having it delivered to my door?

You certainly can! Please make a note of the best date and time for you to pick-up your order so we can ensure that it’s ready and waiting for you.

What if I’m not home when my order is delivered?

That’s no problem at all! Simply leave a cooler with ice outside and we will place your order in there. Please make sure that the delivery is pre-paid and a cooler is visible, otherwise the order will be sent back and available for pick-up.

Can you deliver to apartments and condos?

Our meat can travel to any homestead! Just make sure to include your buzzer code and any details that will be necessary for your order to arrive easily. Our boxes need to stay frozen, so if we are unable to deliver your box, it will be available at our pick-up location:.

What locations do you deliver to?

We deliver to Windsor and surrounding areas. Feel free to contact us if you have any delivery or location questions.
